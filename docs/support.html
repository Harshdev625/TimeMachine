Common Questions

‚ùì How do I install TimeMachine? +

Visit the Chrome Web Store and click "Add to Chrome". The extension will be installed automatically. You can then access it by clicking the TimeMachine icon in your browser toolbar.

üîí Is my browsing data private? +

Yes! TimeMachine only stores domain names and session durations. We never collect full URLs, browsing history, or personal information. All data stays local on your device.

‚ö° How do I start a focus session? +

Click the TimeMachine icon, navigate to the Focus tab, select a preset duration (25, 50, or custom minutes), and click "Start Focus Session". The extension will track your focus time and block distracting sites.

üìä Can I export my time tracking data? +

Yes! Go to the Summary tab and click "Generate PDF Report" to create a comprehensive report with charts and insights. You can also schedule automatic email reports.

üö´ How do I block distracting websites? +

Navigate to the Guard tab, add websites or keywords you want to block, and enable the blocking feature. You can also quick-block the current site with one click.

üé® Can I change the theme? +

Absolutely! TimeMachine offers 7 beautiful themes: Light, Dark, Cyberpunk, Minimal, Ocean, Sunset, and Forest. Go to Settings to switch between themes.

üîÑ How do I sync data across devices? +

Currently, TimeMachine stores data locally on your device. Cloud sync is planned for a future update. You can export your data as a PDF to keep records across devices.

üí∞ Is TimeMachine free? +

Yes! TimeMachine is completely free and open-source under the MIT License. There are no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium features. It will always remain free.

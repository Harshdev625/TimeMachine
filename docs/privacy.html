Privacy Policy

TimeMachine is designed to help you focus while respecting your privacy. We minimize data collection and keep you in control.

What Data We Collect

Category What We Store Storage Location Duration
Account Email for authentication Backend database Until account deletion
Authentication Hashed password Backend database Until account deletion
Time Tracking Domain names and time spent Local device, synced to backend Based on your settings
Focus Sessions Session duration and labels Local device, synced to backend Until you delete them
Blocked Sites Domains and keywords Synced to backend Until you remove them
What We Don't Collect

We never collect full URLs, page content, keystrokes, form data, downloads, or your precise location.

How We Use Your Data

  • Track time spent on websites to generate productivity insights
  • Sync your data across devices when you're signed in
  • Block distracting websites based on your preferences
  • Generate reports and analytics about your browsing habits
Your Data, Your Control

All data processing happens locally first. We only sync what's necessary for cross-device functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising purposes. Your data may only be processed by our infrastructure providers to deliver core features like email reports.

Security Measures

Encrypted Passwords

All passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms

HTTPS Only

All communication is encrypted via secure HTTPS connections

Minimal Data

We only store what's necessary for functionality

Your Rights

Right How to Exercise
View your data Access through extension popup and analytics tab
Delete specific data Use remove buttons in Guard, Focus, and Session tabs
Export your data Generate PDF reports from Summary tab
Delete your account Contact support with your registered email

Cookies & Storage

TimeMachine stores authentication tokens locally using browser storage to keep you signed in. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or analytics services.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through our GitHub repository with an updated date at the top of this page.
